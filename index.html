The Wisconsin and Southern Railroad Unofficial Cheatsheet

T4! WAMX 6030! L595! Watertown Sub! What do these even mean?

The Watco-owned, Class II, 837 mile-long Wisconsin and Southern Railroad has garnished an impressively large following in the land of photographing trains, and understandably many people on various online communities often get confused by its many operations. The goal of this site is to provide accurate information for all to read and use on their next intense WSOR train chase. Good luck to all and stay safe!

If you have any questions or have new or updated information, please click the "CONTACT" button at the top or bottom which will take you to a Google Form. Thank you!
