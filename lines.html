Milwaukee Subdivision Horicon Yard Horicon, WI North Milwaukee Yard Milwaukee, WI T3, T4, T9, T10, L249, L599 MILW Grain, chemicals, lumber, ballast Interchanges with the CN Waukesha Sub in Slinger and Ackerville. The Slinger interchange is in 2 spots, and trains either beefily cross over the CN to continue to/from Milwaukee, or go south onto the CN where WSOR has trackage rights. These rights continue to Waukesha where the Waukesha Subdivision starts. Trains also work the Ackerville Yard, which is another interchange point with CN.

Watertown Subdivision Madison Yard Madison, WI CPKC connection (just before Dayton St) Watertown, WI L464 MILW Grain, plastics, scrap, ballast, chemicals, lumber "There is a quarry east of Waterloo called ""Michaels Quarry"" or ""Michaels Pit"" where there is a spur that comes off into it that contains a mini siding to allow trains to run around its cars. CPKC has trackage rights from that quarry to Watertown and they will periodically send ballast trains from Portage to load/unload ballast at the pit. These are usually more active during the spring and summer and are now usually powered with 2 CP SD60s or ECOs. The symbol used is either I64 or a variant of WWA."
